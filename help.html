Help Page for Pandemic

It is recommended that you keep this page up while you play, so that you can refer to it in order to get command syntax correct.

The Pandemic board consists of three sections -- a map of the world, a section containing player names and their hands, and some extra information and buttons on the right hand side. All moves can be made by clicking on the game page.


To drive, ferry, or shuttle -- Click on the city on the map to which you are moving.

To charter a flight, or take a direct flight -- Click on the card that you will use, then click on the city on the map to which you are moving.

Build a research station -- Operations Experts can click on the Build button. Other players must click on their appropriate card, and then click on the build button.

Heal, Cure, Skip a turn, or Quit -- Click on the appropriate button.

Exchange information -- To take a card, click on the card in another player's hand. To pass a card, click on the card, and then click on the other player's name (located above that player's cards).

Dispatcher -- To move another player, click on that player, then move as instructed above.

Event cards -- Click on the card to activate.

Contingency Planner -- If one of the players is the contingency planner, then a plus sign appears in the lower left corner. If this plus sign is green, clicking on this plus sign will allow the player to reserve a previously used event card.

Other info -- If you are doing a multipart instruction and wish to start over, click the reset button. If you are stuck, then the help button will display more information. The white box in the lower right corner contains special instructions when necessary.
